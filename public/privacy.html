Privacy Policy

Daily Mind Manager respects your privacy. This page explains what data we collect, why, and how you can manage it.

What we collect

  • Local data: mood logs, preferences, pomodoro history — stored in your browser localStorage.
  • Optional analytics: if enabled, anonymized usage analytics may be collected (GA4 or Firebase Analytics).
  • Optional subscriptions: if you provide an email to subscribe to inspirations, it is stored for delivery only.

How we use data

To provide app functionality, sync (if you opt in), send inspiration emails (if you subscribe), and improve the app.

Third-party services

We may use third-party services such as Google AdSense and Google Analytics. Please see their privacy policies for details.

Your choices

You can clear local data from your browser settings. To request deletion of any collected subscriber email, contact the site owner.

Contact

If you have questions about your data or want your subscriber data removed, email: privacy@bitmenders.in

Advertising & Opt-out

We use Google AdSense and Google Analytics when you opt in. To manage ad personalization, visit Google Ads Settings. You can also opt-out via your browser or at adssettings.google.com.
