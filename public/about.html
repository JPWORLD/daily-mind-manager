About Daily Mind Manager

Daily Mind Manager helps you focus with short Pomodoro sessions, capture quick inspirations, and track simple mood trends. It is a privacy-first, offline-capable progressive web app built to be lightweight and helpful.

Why this app

We believe focused time + small habits compound over weeks. This app is intentionally small, with optional analytics and ads to keep it free.
